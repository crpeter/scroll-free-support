Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2025

Scroll Free is designed to protect your attention and your data. We do not collect, store, or sell any personal information. Here's exactly how your privacy is handled:

Scroll Free does not collect any personal information. We do not track your usage, location, contacts, or behavior.

2. No Third-Party Analytics or Ads

Scroll Free does not use analytics tools, advertising SDKs, or third-party trackers of any kind.

3. On-Device Processing

All video editing and content creation happens locally on your device. Nothing is uploaded or analyzed externally.

4. No Login or Accounts

You do not need to sign up or log in to use Scroll Free. We don’t manage any user accounts or passwords.

5. Your Data Stays Yours

Videos you create with Scroll Free remain on your device unless you choose to export or share them.
