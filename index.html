Privacy Policy

We don’t want your data. We don’t store your data. We can’t even see your data.

Scroll Free is designed to work entirely on your device. We don’t use third-party analytics, cloud syncing, or behavioral tracking of any kind.

There are no logins, no account creation, and no identifiers. Your videos never leave your device unless you explicitly share them.

We believe in creative freedom without surveillance — and we back it up in our code.
